About Cheta

Cheta is a cutting-edge decentralized educational tool, combining the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Blockchain to redefine global learning. Our mission is to make education affordable, personalized, and verifiable for everyone.

Our Mission

To democratize education through a subscription-based ecosystem that leverages AI for adaptive learning and blockchain for verifiable credentials. We aim to build a global learning community where knowledge knows no boundaries — secure, personalized, and accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where education is borderless and lifelong — powered by decentralized systems and intelligent learning models. Cheta strives to make education a continuous, affordable experience through a “Spotify-for-learning” model that connects learners, educators, and innovators worldwide.

Our Objectives

Personalized Learning

Use AI to adapt learning paths and provide real-time feedback tailored to each learner’s goals and pace.

Security & Trust

Utilize blockchain for tamper-proof credentials, secure data, and transparent academic records.

Global Access

Connect learners and educators globally through a low-cost subscription model without geographic limits.

Lifelong Learning

Empower continuous skill development with flexible access to micro-courses and professional credentials.

Empower Educators

Give instructors AI tools and blockchain-backed systems to verify contributions and earn fair rewards.

Credential Innovation

Establish a universal framework for decentralized, verifiable certificates accessible worldwide.
